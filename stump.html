High Level Overview

Seeding Round

  • Determines the pecking order for the rest of the tournament
  • (1 game)

Group Stage

  • Players split into 2 groups
  • Winners move to finals
  • (3 games per group)

Finals

  • Winner of this stage WINS THE TOURNAMENT
  • (2 games)

Point System

  • Kill: 1 point
  • Win: 2 points
  • Revenge fuck winner gets the points
  • Blatant disrespect/foul: -1 point
  • If you kill your own nail YOU ARE ELIMINATED FROM THE TOURNAMENT!!! (and must go to bed)

Seeding Round

  • All players play Schnick-Schnak-Schnuk to determine the first thrower.
  • Toast to The Stump
  • Play normal game of stump (no points)
  • Your "seed" is the reverse of your elimination order (ex. If you’re eliminated first, you are the last seed. If you win this game, you are the first seed).
  • The winner of a revenge fuck gets the higher seed
  • Stump A and Stump B are set up
  • In the order of their seeding, players take turns nailing their nails into the stump of their choosing
  • Max of 51% of players on one stump. (ex. If 10 people, 5 per stump. If 9, then one stump has 4, one has 5)

Group Stage

  • 3 games
  • Hammering order of first game is determined by seeding rank. Hammering order of subsequent games determined by elimination order of previous games.
  • At the end of each game, lowest point holder of each stump switch stumps
  • Players accumulate points throughout the group stage
  • All ties are broken by the seeding order
  • 30 minute time limit per game. When time is up, players finish the current round. (in the event of a draw, all remaining players split winners bonus (2 points))
  • 8/9 players
    • Player with most points on each stump advances
    • 2 players with highest point total between groups advance as “wild cards”
  • 10+ players
    • Top 2 players (most points) from each stump advance (4 total)
    • Top three remaining point scorers between the two stumps play a “wild card” game -- winner makes it into the finals. On revenge fuck win, both players make it into the finals.
  • ALL TIES BROKEN BY ORIGINAL SEEDING ORDER

Final

  • Semi-final game
    • Group stage point total determines hammering order (with original seed as tie breaker)
    • 2 Players with lowest point total of this game ARE ELIMINATED
  • Championship
    • Final 3 players
    • Throwing order determined by elimination order from semi finals
    • No time limit
    • Normal revenge fuck rules apply, except for:
      • Revenge fucks can be aimed at ANYONE
      • If you are killed on the revenge fuck, you still get a revenge fuck. (ex. if A kills B, and B kills A on the revenge fuck, A still gets a revenge fuck on C).
      • In the event of a three-way tie, the Championship match goes into overtime.
    • THE WINNER OF THIS GAME IS THE STUMP CHAMPION!
